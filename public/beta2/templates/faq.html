What is this?

TextLab is a virtual communication coach. Type or paste a message (preferably before you send it), and TextLab will let you know how it will be likely perceived by the recipient. You can also use TextLab to analyze messages sent to you. In this way you can analyze the quality of your interactions (soon to be automated).

How do I set the context?

Tap the link, then choose Business or Personal.
